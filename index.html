About Us

  

St. John's is a Gaelic Athletic Association club based in Carraroe and the eastern ward of Sligo, Republic of Ireland. It is one of the newer clubs in the county, having been formed in 1987 after a re-organisation of the GAA structures in the Sligo urban area. Its grounds formerly known as Cuilbeg have been renamed Stenson Park in memory of legendary Sligo player and Curry/St. John's club man Johnny Stenson, they were formally opened in October 2012 by GAA President Liam O'Neill.
